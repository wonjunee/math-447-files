    We are given that the fin has a size of 4 by 4 cm, and we are supposed to find the maximum power that can be dissipated by the fin while keeping the maximum temperature less than 80 degrees celcius.

    Figures on the left and right are showing a fin with K = 1.68 and K = 3.85 respectively.


    The accurate maximum power can be calculated using a bisection method.

    As a result the maximum power at K= 1.68 and K=3.68 are P = 2.269121258165683 W and P = 3.839621224788630 respectively.
